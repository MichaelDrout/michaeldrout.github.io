Michael Drout

Writer, Researcher, Professor of English

About

Drout is the Professor of English and the Director of Medieval Studies at Wheaton College. His research on medieval literature and fantasy is centered around the problems of tradition and influence. He is also part of a Data Mining Group - Wheaton College Lexomics Research Group, where he has been developing new, computer-assisted methods of English text analysis.
